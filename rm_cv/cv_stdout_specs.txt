RoboMasters cv stdout output specifications

Units are in meters and radians. 

color:<blue|red>\n
distance:<64-bit float>\n
y_rotation:<64-bit float>\n
z_rotation:<64-bit float>\n
---\n

$ ./darknet yolo demo
color:blue
distance:1.2345
y_rotation:1.2345
z_rotation:-1.2345

color:red
distance:1.2345

color:blue
